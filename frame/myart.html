About Her

I downloaded Tower of Fantasy largely because of the character creator. I love making original characters, especially trying to create unique characters within somewhat limited character creators. Tower of Fantasy strikes a good balance between allowing enough character customization to make unique characters, but not too much to make it hard to make an appealing character.
