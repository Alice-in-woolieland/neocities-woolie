Date: XX/XX/19 Caption: Old drawing of Robyn.

BIO

-> Robyn Malano
-> He/Him
-> 17/19
-> Filipino
-> Gay
-> Autistic
-> 5'6"
-> Pessimist

Background

He is my oldest OC, made in 2017, and also one of my favorites. His appearance has shifted over the years. He generally stays the same age as me, so his current appearance doesn't nessesarily line up with the portrait above. He is my most "normal" OC, with his life story being pretty relaxed.

Personality

Robyn holds his cards close to his chest. He doesn't like to let others know what he is thinking. People find him kind of intimidating, and for good reason. He doesn't really want to talk to them. He isn't very social, only having a close few friends. With his friends, he is an entirely different person, loud, opinionated, and emotive. He spends his time playing video games, and he likes shooters. He also spends his time talking to his boyfriend, Jax.

History

In elementary school, he was a generally quiet kid, not talking to others. One kid talked to him after learning that Robyn liked video games, and they became good friends. Oliver was essentially Robyn's only friend all the way till highschool. Robyn never stopped Oliver from talking to others, since Oliver was relatively popular, maintaining many friendships, but Oliver always spent lunch with Robyn. Oliver was the first person to learn that Robyn was gay, back in middle school. In highschool, Robyn meets Jax, a new kid from Arizona. Robyn likes Jax almost instantly, a fact Oliver picks up on. Eventually, Robyn asks Jax out, and the rest is history.
